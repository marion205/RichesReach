## üé® **DESIGN GUIDELINES**

### **Overall Style**
- **Minimal text** (max 3-4 bullet points per slide)
- **Big numbers** (make them pop)
- **High contrast** (readable from across table)
- **Consistent branding** (logo on every slide)

### **Color Palette**
- Primary: Deep blue/navy (#0066FF or #0A0E27)
- Accent: Bright cyan/gold (#00D9FF or #FFD700)
- Background: White or dark (pick one, stay consistent)
- Text: High contrast (black on white, white on dark)

### **Typography**
- **Headlines:** Bold, 36-48pt
- **Body:** Regular, 18-24pt
- **Numbers:** Extra bold, 60pt+
- **Font:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, Montserrat)

### **Visuals**
- **Icons:** Simple, consistent style
- **Charts:** Minimal, easy to read
- **Photos:** High quality, relevant
- **Mockups:** Clean, professional

## üéØ **DELIVERY TIPS**

### **Opening (Slide 1)**
- Start with the big number ($1.2T)
- Pause for impact
- Make eye contact

### **Middle (Slides 2-7)**
- Keep it conversational
- Ask: "Does this resonate?" or "Have you seen this problem?"
- Adjust based on their interest

### **Close (Slides 8-10)**
- Be clear about the ask
- Leave room for questions
- Have next steps ready

### **Questions to Expect**
- "How is this different from Robinhood?"
- "What's your traction?"
- "Who are your competitors?"
- "What's your business model?"
- "How much are you raising?"

**Have 1-2 sentence answers ready for each.**
