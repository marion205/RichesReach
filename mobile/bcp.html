Business Continuity Plan (BCP)

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Overview

RichesReach AI is committed to maintaining continuous service availability for our users. This Business Continuity Plan outlines our procedures for maintaining operations during and after a disruption.

2. Service Availability

We strive to maintain 99.9% uptime for our core services. Our infrastructure is designed with redundancy and failover capabilities to minimize service interruptions.

3. Infrastructure

3.1 Cloud Infrastructure

  • Hosting: Amazon Web Services (AWS) ECS
  • Database: AWS RDS PostgreSQL with automated backups
  • Caching: AWS ElastiCache Redis
  • Storage: AWS S3 with versioning and cross-region replication

3.2 Redundancy

  • Multi-AZ (Availability Zone) deployment
  • Automated failover capabilities
  • Daily automated database backups
  • Data replication across regions

4. Disaster Recovery Procedures

4.1 Data Backup

  • Database backups: Daily automated backups with 30-day retention
  • Application code: Version controlled in Git repositories
  • Configuration: Stored in secure, version-controlled systems

4.2 Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)

  • Critical Services: 4 hours
  • Non-Critical Services: 24 hours

4.3 Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)

  • Database: 1 hour (point-in-time recovery available)
  • User Data: 24 hours

5. Incident Response

5.1 Monitoring

We continuously monitor our systems using AWS CloudWatch, application performance monitoring (APM), and error tracking (Sentry) to detect issues early.

5.2 Escalation Procedures

  • Level 1: Automated alerts and self-healing systems
  • Level 2: Engineering team notification
  • Level 3: Executive escalation for critical issues

6. Communication

In the event of a service disruption, we will communicate updates through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Status page: status.richesreach.com
  • Email notifications to affected users
  • Social media updates (if applicable)

7. Third-Party Dependencies

Our service depends on the following third-party providers:

  • Yodlee: Bank account linking services
  • Alpaca: Brokerage and trading services
  • AWS: Cloud infrastructure
  • Market Data Providers: Finnhub, Polygon, Alpha Vantage

We monitor the status of these services and have contingency plans for service interruptions.

8. Testing and Maintenance

We regularly test our disaster recovery procedures through:

  • Quarterly disaster recovery drills
  • Monthly backup restoration tests
  • Continuous monitoring and alerting

10. Updates to This Plan

This Business Continuity Plan is reviewed and updated annually, or more frequently as needed based on changes to our infrastructure or business operations.
