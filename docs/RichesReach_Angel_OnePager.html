RichesReach

One-page overview for angel investors

The Problem

Most people lack the confidence and clarity to invest. They’re overwhelmed by jargon, don’t know where to start, and either avoid investing or make emotional decisions. Traditional apps focus on execution, not understanding—so users trade without learning.

Our Solution

RichesReach is a learning-first investing app that meets users where they are: plain-English daily briefs, 2‑minute lessons, and AI-guided next steps—backed by real portfolio and market data.

  • Daily Brief — Personalized market summary + one clear action with optional 2‑min lessons.
  • Lesson Library — Bite-sized lessons (stocks, bonds, risk, tax) with progress and streaks.
  • Portfolio + Trading — Real brokerage connectivity, paper trading, and live positions.
  • Voice & AI — “Hey Riches” voice assistant, daily voice digest, and AI insights.
  • Security — Encrypted broker tokens, compliance-aware architecture.

Why Now

Retail investing and self-directed learning are the norm. AI and voice make personalized education scalable. Broker and market-data APIs make “learn + invest” products buildable and compliant.

Traction & Product

Mobile (React Native) + backend (Django/FastAPI). Lesson library and daily brief on real market data; broker integration and paper trading live. Market data facade, encryption, and performance work in place. Roadmap: deeper AI insights, behavioral analytics, expanded content.

Market

Target: Everyday investors who want to learn while they invest. TAM: U.S. retail investing + financial literacy (multi-billion $).
