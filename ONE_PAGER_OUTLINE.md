## üé® **DESIGN NOTES**

### **Typography**
- **Headline:** Bold, sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar) - 48-60pt
- **Body:** Clean, readable (Inter, Open Sans) - 12-14pt
- **Numbers:** Extra bold, large (72pt+)

### **Color Palette Options**

**Option 1: Dark Premium**
- Background: Deep navy/black (#0A0E27 or #000000)
- Accent: Bright cyan/blue (#00D9FF or #0066FF)
- Text: White/light gray
- Numbers: Gold/yellow (#FFD700)

**Option 2: Clean White**
- Background: White
- Accent: Bold blue/purple (#0066FF or #7B2CBF)
- Text: Dark gray/black
- Numbers: Accent color

**Option 3: Gradient**
- Background: Gradient (blue to purple)
- Accent: White/gold
- Text: White
- Numbers: Gold/yellow

### **Visual Hierarchy**
1. **Headline** (biggest, boldest)
2. **Numbers** (second biggest, colored)
3. **Problem/Solution** (medium, clear contrast)
4. **Features** (smaller, icons help)
5. **Contact** (smallest but clear)

### **Whitespace**
- Generous padding (don't cram)
- Clear sections with breathing room
- Visual breaks between sections
